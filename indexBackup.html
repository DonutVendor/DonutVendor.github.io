A Little About Us


Dwarven Realms Studio is run by Devon Mickels and Jason Hearne. Since then, he has been creating content in his free time, mostly centered around that of the gaming industry. While banging on his keyboard all day might sound like loads of fun, he also finds joy in outdoor activities such as rockclimbing and snowboarding!


While the majority of his projectes are focused around mobile games, Devon also develops a projects for PC. All projects created by Dwarven Realms Studios are hosted here. Be sure to keep up with our Dev Blog to keep up to date with what were up to!
