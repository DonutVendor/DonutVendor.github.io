New features ahoy!


Welcome back to another update of Exhiled! Be sure to follow us on twitter for notifications of when our Devblogs go live!. This week we focused on adding the new resource 'Firewood'. Citizens will now require warmth! Houses, if stocked with firewood, will continuously generate heat from Early fall till Spring. When citizens are in need of warmth, and are not homeless, will navigate their way home and rest in their home for a bit while they warm up next to the cozy fire.

Firewood is produced by the 'Wood Cutter'.
1 Log turns in to 4 Firewood.
Smoke can be seen coming from the chimney of any house generating heat.
