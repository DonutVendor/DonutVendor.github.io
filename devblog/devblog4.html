Time for college


Its move in week! Thats right, this week I moved into my dorm at college, which as you could imagine, lead to me not getting that much done. What little time I had in between finishing my job and packing, I worked hard on clearing up lots of bugs from the legacy version of Operation Blood Count, as well as continuing to work on the first map. I've touched up on the spider animation, as well as added a new mob, the "Ticker".(More to come) Dont expect much to come this week, as it will be my first week in college and I will find myself spending more time adjusting and getting to know others, rather then typing away at my keyboard. But dont worry, after this week I should be back to my pace working on both Exhiled and Operation Blood Count! Ill see you then!
