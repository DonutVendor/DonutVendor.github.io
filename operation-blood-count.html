Currently undergoing a complete overhaul

This project has bounced back and forth among my interests. Originally, I had planned it to be a mobile game but lost interest part way through. In an attempt to revive the project, I swapped it over to a PC release, but no longer enjoyed the direction it was heading. So, after much thought, I have switched it back to a mobile platform, bringing back the good old top down aspect! With some new artwork, and a rework of controls, you should be able to expect an early alpha to be coming soon!
