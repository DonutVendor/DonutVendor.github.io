Multiplication Fun


Train your brain and learn your multiplication table!














This project was part of a game in 24 hours contest!

Multiplication Fun aims to help you learn your multiplication table! Get better, and faster at multiplying while playing a fun little game! Get points for getting answers correct, and addition points as you continuously select the correct answer!

Track your score to see your progress, and sooner or later you'll have your multiplication table down! Math has never been so easy!
