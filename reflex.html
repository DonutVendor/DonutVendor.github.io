Reflex


Compete against friends to see who really has the best reflex skills!














This project was part of a game in 24 hours contest!

Do your reflexes have what it takes to score the highest? Can you keep track of each space between the circles and react when its time? Then this is the perfect app for you! Download Reflex and jump right into the game!

With four circles spinning among each other, your job is to click the empty space before time runs out! Its that easy! How high can you score? Try today!
